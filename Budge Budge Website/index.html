Background

Budge Budge Refineries Ltd is a major player in the medium scale industries sector. It was incorporated in the year 1981 as a Public Limited Company and was acquired by the present management in the year 2004 as sick unit. The plant is Fully based on Imported raw material to feed the local market.

The plant is fully indigenous based on Holland and Belgium technology to produce international quality.

The plant is installed for -

  • Refining
  • Fractionation
  • Vanaspati with Bakery
